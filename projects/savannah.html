Mosquito Savannah

Date: April-May 2024

Duration: Over 200hrs

Home

In the game, the player's goal is to help reduce Malaria in a nearby village. They test different ponds for mosquito larvae and make larvicide from plants they find.

They also gather plants to make Malaria medicine. Finally, they use a robot to deliver the medicine to the village. Thus reducing Malaria

Fun Fact: the character swimming is actually the in air animation,which is triggered when the player is off the ground. The script raises the player a little when they collide with a plane inside the ponds.

Mechanics:

The Character is from the Unity Asset Store from the package, Starter Asset Pack

The cart follows the character around serving as a carrier and lights the character's way at night.

It uses the Nav mesh AI

There is a day and night system, where when it gets to dusks the lights including those on the cart light up

Mosquitos are in form of a particle system that only plays when the player is closeby

The Pond and station markers look at the player using the "look at" component to allow legibility

Steps taken to Increase fps(frames per second)

  • Qulity settings are set to High quality, instead of ultra high
  • Camera culling on static objects.
  • Some textures and meshes are compressed.
  • Used a unity asset that reduces LOD(levelof detail in plants).
  • Used Baked lights.
  • Use LookAt Constraint component for makers to facetheplayer instead of a script.
  • Moved the sky calculations to a coroutine instead of the update().
  • In place of Canvas where nothing changes eg. the station markers, i used sprites with the words and images on them.
  • Particle systems have a trigger script that plays them when the player is near and stops when the player is away.
  • Avoided the use of mesh colliders and opted for built in mesh like cubes n spheres coliders.
